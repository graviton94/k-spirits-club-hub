# 2026-04-14 Work Log: AI Sommelier & UI Stabilization

## 1. Engine & Data Strategy (Live Firestore Sync)
*   **Transition to Live Data**: Abandoned the stale `ingested-data.json` local file. The system now cross-references AI suggestions with the **live Firebase Production DB** in real-time.
*   **AI Sommelier Logic Refinement**:
